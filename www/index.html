About Fnatic

Fnatic is a leading organisation in eSports, with professional teams in the most popular games such as: League of Legends, Dota 2, Battlefield 4, and more.

History

The Fnatic organisation was founded by both Sam Mathews and Anne Mathews. Fnatic's HQ is located in the heart of London, United Kingdom. With offices in Belgrade, Serbia and a professional gaming house in Cologne, Germany. While staying true to our roots Fnatic will continue to breed the best talent, select the finest partners, and continue to exceed expectations.
